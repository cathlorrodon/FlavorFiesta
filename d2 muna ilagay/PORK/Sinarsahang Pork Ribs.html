Sinarsahang Pork Ribs Recipe

What is Sinarsahang Pork Ribs?

Rich and oozing with a deep savory flavor, Sinarsahang Pork Ribs is definitely a dish I would recommend to anyone hoping to really make the most of their pork ribs. This type of meat is really recognized for its tender and meaty taste that collaborates well with a somewhat salty and velvety sauce. That’s where our sarsa comes in!

Ingredients

3 lbs. pork ribs (note 1)

1 Knorr Pork cube

12 string beans (note 2)

1 carrot (note 3)

3 ounces tomato paste

3 tablespoons soy sauce

1 onion chopped

5 cloves garlic minced

2 tablespoons butter

½ teaspoon white sugar

2 cups water

3 tablespoons cooking oil

Salt and ground black pepper to taste

Instructions / How to Cook

1. Steam string beans and carrots for 3 minutes. Set aside.

2. Heat the cooking oil in a pan. Sauté the onion and garlic until the onion softens.

3. Put the pork ribs in the pan. Sauté until it turns light brown.

4. Pour the soy sauce, add tomato sauce, and water. Let boil.

5. Add Knorr Pork Cube. Cover the pan. Simmer until the pork tenderizes completely.

6. Add the butter. Continue simmering uncovered until the sauce reduces to half.

7. Add sugar and season with salt and ground black pepper.

8. Transfer to a serving plate. Serve with steamed vegetables.

9. Serve. Share and enjoy!
