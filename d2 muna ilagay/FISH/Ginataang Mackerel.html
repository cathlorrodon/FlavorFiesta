Ginataang Mackerel

What is Ginataang Mackerel?

Ginataang Mackerel with Spinach is a simple dish that can be eaten for lunch. It is composed of Canned Mackerel in brine, coconut milk, and fresh spinach. This budget-friendly recipe is simple and straightforward. It only takes less than 20 minutes to complete the entire dish.

Ingredients

30 ounces mackerel canned in brine

1 bunch spinach

2 cups coconut milk

5 pieces Thai chili pepper optional

3 thumbs ginger sliced into thin strips

1 piece onion chopped

5 cloves garlic minced

½ cup water

¼ cup white vinegar

3 tablespoons cooking oil

Fish sauce and ground black pepper to taste

Instructions / How to Cook

1. Heat oil in a cooking pot.

2. Saute onion, garlic, and ginger until garlic browns and onion softens.

3. Pour coconut milk into the cooking pot. Add chili pepper.Stir. Cover the pot and cook for 5 minutes.

4. Add mackerel. Pour water and vinegar into the pot. Let boil. Stir and cover the pot. Cook for 5 to 7 minutes.

5. Season with fish sauce and ground black pepper.

6. Add spinach. Cook for 1 minute.

7. Transfer to a serving plate. Serve.

8. Share and enjoy!
