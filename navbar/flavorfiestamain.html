Mechadong Baboy

Mechadong Baboy or Pork mechado is a type of Filipino pork stew. It is cooked in a tomato-based sauce.

Adobong Baboy

It is a dish composed of pork slices cooked in soy sauce, vinegar, and garlic. Popular dish in PH.

Tinolang Manok

A great tasting and healthy dish to prepare. It is easy to cook and friendly on the budget.

Cordon Bleu

A dish composed of chicken breasts, ham, and Swiss cheese. Rolled together then dredged in bread crumbs.

Dynamite Lumpia

A version of the popular Filipino deep-fried egg rolls. It involves the use of stuffed long green chili.
