Sisig Paella Recipe

What is Sisig Paella?

This recipe is the answer to your simultaneous cravings for the ever so savory sisig, and the rich paella. Sisig uses a lot of parts of the pig, which we then mince for a meal that’s both easy to eat, and is just absolutely delicious. We’ve got the flavors of onions and chili peppers for some spice that isn’t too heavy.

Ingredients

1 lb. pork belly grilled and diced

1 Knorr Pork Cube

1 chorizo de Bilbao diced

5 quail eggs

2 cups paella rice

3 teaspoons Knorr Liquid Seasoning

3 ½ cups water

¼ cups green onions chopped

2 teaspoons smoked paprika

2 long green pepper sliced

¼ cup red bell pepper chopped

1 onion minced

4 cloves garlic minced

3 tablespoons cooking oil

lemon wedged

Dressing Ingredients:

¾ cup Lady’s Choice Mayonnaise

2 ½ teaspoons Knorr Liquid Seasoning

Instructions / How to Cook

1. Heat oil. Sauté onion and garlic until the onion softens.

2. Add chorizo, pork belly, and Knorr Pork Cube. Sauté for 1 minute.

3. Add the paella rice. Cook for 1 minute while stirring.

4. Pour the water into the pan and add Knorr Liquid Seasoning and bell pepper. Stir. Cover the pot. Continue cooking until the rice is done.

5. Sprinkle green onions on top. Crack the eggs and top over the rice. Add long green pepper. Cover and cook for 2 minutes.

6. Combine the dressing ingredients and drizzle over the sisig paella. Serve with lemon wedges.

7. Share and enjoy!
