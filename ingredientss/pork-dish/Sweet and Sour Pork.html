Sweet and Sour Pork Recipe

What is Sweet and Sour Pork?

This Sweet and Sour Pork Recipe is all that you need to make a good Sweet and Sour Pork dish. It is simple, straightforward, and easy to follow. The result is absolutely delicious. You should try it for yourself.

Ingredients

1/2 kilo pork-loin, cut into pieces

1 small can Pineapple chunks

1 large Onion, cut into quartered

1 green bell peppers, cut into strips

1 red bell peppers, cut into strips

2 tbsps. Soy sauce

3 tbsps. Canola oil

4 tbsps. Cornstarch

3 tbsps. Tomato ketchup

1 tbsp. sugar

1 tsp. Salt

1/4 cup white Vinegar

3 cloves Garlic, minced

2 cups Water

Instructions / How to Cook

1. In a deep pan, add pork and pour 2 cups of water, add salt, then apply heat and bring to a boil until the pork is tender, then drain and set aside the stock.

2. In a separate wide bowl add soy sauce and cornstarch, mix together then coat the pork.

3. Fry the coated pork in a frying pan until golden brown then set aside.

4. In a 1/2 cup of Pork stock dissolve the remaining cornstarch then set aside.

5. In another pot, add oil then saute onions and garlic.

6. Add-in pineapple chunks including the syrup, bell peppers, , ketchup, sugar, vinegar, dissolved cornstarch, and salt

7. Stir well until the sauce thickens.

8. Place the fried pork in a platter then pour-in the sweet and sour sauce.

9. Best served with Hot steamed rice
