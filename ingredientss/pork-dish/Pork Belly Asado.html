Pork Belly Asado Recipe

What is Pork Belly Asado?

Pork Belly Asado with tender pork belly cubes and colorful vegetables in a rich tomato-based sauce is hearty, tasty, and perfect with steamed rice. It’s easy to make and sure to be a family favorite. Perfect for the holidays! Asado refers to two kinds of Filipino braised meat dishes. The Chinese-style version has a sweet and savory flavor and is made by braising the pork in soy sauce, brown sugar, aromatics, and Chinese spices.

Ingredients

3 lbs. pork belly

1 Knorr Pork Cube

¼ cup banana ketchup

3 tablespoons soy sauce

3 tablespoons brown sugar

2 teaspoons cornstarch

8 cloves garlic minced

1 onion minced

2 star anise

2 tablespoons butter

4 cups water

3 tablespoons cooking oil

Instructions / How to Cook

1. Combine pork belly, ketchup, soy sauce, and garlic. Mix well. Marinate overnight.

2. Heat oil in a pot. Saute onion until it softens.

3. Add marinated pork. Saute until it browns.

4. Pour water into the pan. Let it boil.

5. Add Knorr Pork cube and star anise. Cover and continue cooking for 1 hour.

6. Add brown sugar and butter. Stir.

7. Make a slurry by combining cornstarch with ¼ cup water. Mix well. Pour the mixture into the pot. Cook until the sauce thickens.

8. Season with salt and ground black pepper.

9. Serve with rice. Share and enjoy!
