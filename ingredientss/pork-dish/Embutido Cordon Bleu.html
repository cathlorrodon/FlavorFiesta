Embutido Cordon Bleu Recipe

What is Embutido Cordon Bleu?

Cordon bleu is undeniably a delicious dish as it is what with its irresistible cheesiness, and its satisfying crispy texture on the outside. When you combine two popular dishes, you do run the risk of having it not work out. This could be due to contrasting flavors, or cooking processes that are far too different from each other.

Ingredients

8 slices ham

8 slices Swiss cheese

½ cup all-purpose flour

2 eggs

1 1/2 cups panko breadcrumbs

2 cups cooking oil

Embutido Ingredients:

1 lb. ground pork

1 lb. ground chicken

1 Knorr Chicken Cube mashed

¼ cup sweet pickle relish

1 cup cheddar cheese grated

1 red bell pepper minced

½ cup raisins

1 carrot minced

1 onion minced

1 egg

1 teaspoons salt

1/2 teaspoon ground black pepper

3/4 cups breadcrumbs

Ground black pepper to taste

Dijon Sauce:

½ cup heavy whipping cream

2 tablespoons Dijon mustard

1 ½ tablespoons butter

2 tablespoons minced yellow onion

Salt to taste

Instructions / How to Cook

1. Combine all the embutido ingredients in a bowl. Mix well. Set aside.

2. Lay a sheet of foil on a flat surface. Scoop ¾ cups of meat mixture and spread it on the foil. Lay 2 slices of ham over the meat mixture and top it with 2 slices of cheese. Roll the foil and secure by twisting the ends.

3. Steam for 1 hour. Remove from the steamer and let this cool down. Remove foil wrap.

4. Heat 2 cups cooking oil.

5. Dredge each embutido in all-purpose flour. Dip it in beaten egg, and then coat with panko breadcrumbs.

6. Deep fry until golden brown. Set aside.

7. Make the Dijon cream sauce by melting butter in a saucepan. Add onion. Cook until soft.

8. Pour heavy whipping cream. Stir and add Dijon mustard. Stir and continue cooking using between low to medium heat setting until the sauce reduces to half. Add salt to taste.

9. Arrange the embutido cordon bleu on a serving plate. Serve with creamy Dijon sauce. Share and enjoy
