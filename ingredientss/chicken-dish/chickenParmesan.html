Chicken Parmesan

What is Chicken Parmesan?

Chicken Parmesan, also known as Chicken Parmigiana, is a popular Italian-American dish that has become a staple in many households and restaurants.

Ingredients

2 large chicken breasts (about 1 1/2 lbs)

1/2 cup all-purpose flour

1 teaspoon salt

1/2 teaspoon black pepper

2 large eggs (beaten until frothy)

1/2 cup Italian bread crumbs

1/2 cup Parmesan cheese (shredded or grated)

2 tablespoons light olive oil (to saute)

24 ounces marinara sauce

4 ounces mozzarella cheese (1 cup shredded)

1 tablespoon fresh basil (or parsley, chopped for optional garnish)

Instructions / How to Cook

1. Preheat oven to 400Â°F.

2. Cut chicken breasts in half horizontally to make 4 thin cutlets.

3. Season chicken with salt and pepper.

4. Dredge chicken in flour, shaking off excess.

5. Dip chicken in egg, then coat in bread crumbs and Parmesan cheese.

6. Heat olive oil in a large skillet over medium-high heat.

7. Add chicken and cook until golden brown on both sides.

8. Transfer chicken to a baking dish.

9. Pour marinara sauce over chicken.

10. Sprinkle mozzarella cheese over sauce.

11. Bake for 15-20 minutes or until cheese is melted and bubbly.

12. Garnish with fresh basil or parsley.
