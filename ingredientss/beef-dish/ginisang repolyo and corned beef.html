Corn Beef Guisado with Potato Recipe

What is Ginisang Repolyo and Corned Beef?

Corned Beef with Repolyo Recipe is tasty and delicious. This dish is one of the many canned corned beef makeovers that we offer to you with a healthy twist.

Ingredients

2 cans 150g corned beef

2 cups sliced cabbage

1 small onion sliced

1 clove minced garlic

1 tablespoons cooking oil

Salt and ground black pepper to taste

Instructions / How to Cook

1. Heat oil in a frypan then saute garlic and onion.

2. Add corned beef, then simmer for 3 minutes.

3. Add sliced Repolyo (cabbage), then cover and continue to simmer for 5 minutes or until cabbage are soft.

4. Season with salt and pepper then mix.

5. If you want it dry, just continue to simmer.

6. Transfer to a serving dish and serve with rice.
