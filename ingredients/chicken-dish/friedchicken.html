Fried Chicken With Mayo Garlic Glaze Recipe

What is Fried Chicken With Mayo Garlic Glaze?

Fried Chicken with Mayo Garlic Glaze is a savory and creamy dish that is perfect for those who love the rich taste of garlic. The dish is made by marinating chicken thighs in a mixture of Knorr Liquid Seasoning, ground black pepper, and garlic powder. The chicken is then coated in a flour mixture and deep-fried until golden brown. The glaze is made by combining mayonnaise, lemon juice, honey, and garlic. The glaze is then brushed onto the fried chicken, giving it a unique flavor that is both salty and sour.

Ingredients

1 ½ lbs. chicken thighs cut into serving pieces

2 eggs beaten

2 cups cooking oil

Chicken Seasoning Ingredients

2 tablespoons Knorr Liquid Seasoning

½ teaspoon ground black pepper

1 teaspoon garlic powder

Flour Mixture Ingredients

¾ cup all-purpose flour

½ cup cornstarch

½ teaspoon salt

1 teaspoon onion powder

Glaze Ingredients

1 tablespoon butter

3 cloves garlic minced

½ cup Lady’s Choice Mayonnaise

½ teaspoon lemon juice

2 tablespoons honey

1 teaspoon Knorr Liquid Seasoning

Instructions / How to Cook

1. Combine chicken with the chicken seasoning ingredients. Mix well. Let it stay for 10 minutes.

2. Meanwhile combine flour mixture ingredients. Mix well and set aside.

3. Heat 2 cups of cooking oil in a deep cooking pot.

4. While the oil is heating-up, dredge the chicken in flour mixture. Dip in beaten egg, and then dredge once more with the flour mixture.

5. Deep-fry the chicken for 14 minutes or until golden brown. Remove chicken from the cooking pot. Set aside.

6. Make the Mayo Garlic glaze by melting butter on a clean pan. Add garlic. Cook until it starts to brown. Turn the heat off and add Lady’s Choice Mayonnaise, lemon juice, honey, and Knorr Liquid Seasoning. Mix well.

7. Add the crispy fried chicken and toss with the glaze until completely coated.

8. Transfer to a serving plate. Serve. Share and enjoy!
