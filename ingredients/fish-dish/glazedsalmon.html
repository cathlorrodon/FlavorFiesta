Easy Glazed Salmon

What is Easy Glazed Salmon?

This glazed salmon recipe comes together in just 20 minutes, and in one pan. Hands down, the most addicting salmon ever! This ultra easy salmon recipe is a winner when it comes to weeknight dinners!

Ingredients

1/3 cup packed brown sugar

1/4 cup unsweetened pineapple juice

2 tablespoons soy sauce

4 salmon fillets (6 ounces each)

Instructions / How to Cook

1. Line a 15x10x1-in. baking pan with foil; grease the foil. In a small bowl, combine the brown sugar, pineapple juice and soy sauce. Place salmon skin side down on prepared pan. Spoon sauce mixture over fish.

2. Bake, uncovered, at 350Â° for 20-25 minutes or until fish flakes easily with a fork, basting frequently with pan juices.
