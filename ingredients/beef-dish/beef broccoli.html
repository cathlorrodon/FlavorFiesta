Beef Broccoli Recipe

What is Beef Broccoli?

Beef Broccoli is an Asian beef main dish. As the name suggests, this dish is mainly comprised of beef and broccoli combined with different sauces for that delightful Asian flavor.

Ingredients

300 g Beef Strips thin & pounded

1 tbsp Sesame Oil

1 tbsp Corn Starch

1/4 tsp Black Pepper

360 g Broccoli trimmed & cut

1 tsp Salt

1 liter Water for boiling

2 cups Icy Water for soak & rinse

1 med Red Bell Pepper julienne

2 tbsp Oil for saute

1 cup Chicken Stock or Water

2 tbsp Soy Sauce

2 tbsp Oyster Sauce

1 tbsp Sesame Oil

2 tbsp Corn Starch or Potato Starch

1-2 tsp Sugar

1/4 tsp Black Pepper to taste

Instructions / How to Cook

1. Pond the thinly sliced 300g Beef Strips.

2. In large bowl season Beef with 2tbsp. Soy Sauce, 1/4tsp. Black Pepper, 1tbsp. Corn Starch and 1tbsp. Sesame Oil.

3. Massage beef to season evenly then, let it rest for at least an hour to marinate well.

4. In another bowl combine Chicken Stock, Soy Sauce, Black Pepper and Sesame Oil.

5. Add Sugar, Oyster Sauce and Corn Starch.

6. Stir well until starch is fully dissolve then set aside.

7. In a pot add 1tbsp. Salt into a Liter of Water & bring to a boil on med. heat.

8. Next, when it boils add Broccoli in a boiling water.

9. In another bowl add ice cubes in 2 cups of water.

10. Blanch Broccoli for about 1-2 minutes.

11. Next, transfer Broccoli into the cold water to stop the cooking process.

12. Drain well & Set aside.

13. Heat 2 tbsp. Oil in a frying pan & saute Red Bell Pepper.

14. Saute for about 2-3 minutes & remove from the pan.

15. On the same pan add 1tbsp. Oil & fry marinated Beef on a med. high heat.

16. Fry Beef for about 4-5 minutes, (We don't want to over cook it.)

17. Stir the sauce & pour onto the beef.

18. Let it simmer and stir until the sauce thickens.

19. Add the blanched Broccoli florettes.

20. Add sauteed Red Bell Peppers.

21. Stir just until well blended & coated.

22. Our Stir Fried Beef Broccoli with Bell Pepper is done!

23. Garnish with Fried Garlic or Toasted Sesame Seeds.

24. This super easy to cook Beef recipe is great for your family.

25. Serve hot with steamed rice.
